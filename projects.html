Finding Terrain-Specific Layout of a Heliostat Field

Summary: Efficient computational methods for heliostat field design on uneven terrain.

Objective:

  • Accurate estimation of optical efficiency of a heliostat field at cheaper computational cost
  • Optimize layout for complex terrain using heuristic techniques

Method:

  • Two-level identification of potenial shaders and blockers
  • Edge-to-center approach for estimating shading and blocking
  • Using combined scheme of pseudo ray tracing and elliptical gaussian to estimate spillage
  • Applying GA and PSO to optimize heliostat field using advanced objective function

Key Contributions:

  • Reduced computational cost
  • Accurate terrain-aware modeling
  • Optimizing using advanced objective function

Tools: Python, Numerical modeling
